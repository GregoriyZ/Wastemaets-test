Our services

One crew, one truck, almost anything gone. Residential and commercial rubbish removal across Melbourne and the surrounding areas.

RUBBISH REMOVAL

Household, commercial and general junk cleared in a single trip — bags, boxes, furniture and everything in between. We do all the loading and the tip runs.

TENANT CLEAN OUTS

End-of-lease and vacate clean-outs done fast — get your bond back and the keys handed over without the stress. Ideal for renters, agents and property managers.

YARD CLEAN UPS

Overgrown gardens, prunings, clippings, soil and storm debris cleared out — we'll leave your yard neat and tidy and divert green waste to mulch where we can.

LIGHT DEMOLITION

Fences, sheds, pergolas and decking knocked down and hauled away — debris removed and the site swept clean, ready for whatever comes next.

DECEASED ESTATES

Respectful, discreet estate clear-outs handled with care and the flexibility families need during a difficult time. We can donate and recycle on your behalf.

HARD & BULKY WASTE

Old lounges, mattresses, fridges and washing machines lifted out and hauled away — no item too heavy or awkward, and no stairs too tricky.
