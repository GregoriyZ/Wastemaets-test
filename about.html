Our story

Built on doing it right.

WasteMates started with a simple frustration: rubbish removal that turned up late, quoted vague and left a mess behind. We reckoned Melbourne deserved better — a crew that shows up on time, gives an honest price, and treats your place like their own.

Today we clear everything from a single mattress to whole-house and commercial jobs across Melbourne and the surrounding areas. We hand-load it all, sort what can be recycled, and sweep up before we go.

No call centres, no runaround — just a friendly local team and a tipper truck, ready when you are.
